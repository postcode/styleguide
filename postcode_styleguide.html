PostCode Style Guide

PostCode is a mission driven company that provides technology applications and services to local governments. We focus on open source software and user-centered design. Our goal is to help the public sector operate more efficiently while engaging residents more effectively.

This style guide is to be used as a starting point for any visual design undertaken at PostCode and especially for all design and front end development of websites and applications. It is intended to be used by PostCode internally and in collaboration with any future partners.

Images

As part of it's brand identity PostCode uses images heavily, particularly images that depict people and environments that are not fabricated. In addition, as part of its brand position, PostCode will use Creative Commons commercially licensed images by deafult, making sure to give proper attribution. (Flickr.com has a large selection, which you can filter accordingly). PostCode will always use high quality images - those with high resolution, good composition, and good lighting. When no high quality Creative Commons images can be found licensed images are acceptable.
